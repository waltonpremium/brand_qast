APIs

Similar to syndicating your product information and pricing to downstream subscribers, you can also pubish your data to APIs that allow other systems to pull your product data programatically. You can create a single API that exposes all of your product data or many APIs to segment your data for different purposes or for different vendor needs. When you create an API you'll have complete control over which product and pricing information is published in your API.
