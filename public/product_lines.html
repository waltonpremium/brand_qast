Product Lines

It looks like you haven't created any product lines yet! Simply put product lines are the highest level grouping of products in your organization's Brandqast account. Your company may only need to create a single product line for all products or you may get more complex and have different product lines for product types or create a new one for each quarter or year. Product lines also allow you to control which products are syndicated to your downstream subscribers and how data is published via APIs.
